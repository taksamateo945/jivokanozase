TEEN Mom Kailyn Lowry responded to rumors she’s currently on vacation with her third baby daddy Chris Lopez.

The reality star is living it up in the Dominican Republic along with her four sons.

During a Q&A on Instagram, Kailyn received plenty of inquires, including one from a fan that read: “Are the rumors true, is Chris on vacay with you?”

However, the MTV star simply shared a selfie of herself in response with the words: “Hell no.”

The former pair, who share 3-year-old Lux and 11-month-old Creed, haven’t exactly seen eye-to-eye since parting ways, as they have a rough past.

Most recently, the exes were involved in quite the shouting match on an episode of Teen Mom 2, which had Kailyn yelling at Chris, and the father of two swearing a ton at MTV producers.

A ROUGH PAST

Last year, Kailyn was arrested after “punching Chris several times with a closed fist” over their eldest child’s haircut.

In February, The Sun exclusively revealed that the charges against Kailyn were dropped.

Recently, Kailyn filed a lawsuit against fellow costar Briana DeJesus claiming that the mother of two “asserted that Lowry physically beat Christopher Lopez, the father of two of Lowry’s sons, and broke into and entered the home of Mr. Lopez’s mother.”

According to court documents obtained by The Sun, Kailyn’s legal team claimed that Briana’s comments are false and were made “for the purpose of causing Lowry harm… Defendant used Lowry to gain additional media attention for herself.”
