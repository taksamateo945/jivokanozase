Career

Nick Young played at the University of California from 2004 to 2007, after beginning his career at Cleveland High School. During that time, he was named to the best playing XI for two years in a row. Following his great undergraduate career, he was picked by the Washington Wizards with the 16th overall selection in the NBA, where he played from 2007 to 2012. He set a career-best with 43 points against the ‘Sacramento Kings’ in 2011.

Furthermore, he signed with the Los Angeles Lakers from 2013 to 2017, before spending one year with the Los Angeles Clippers and Philadelphia 76ers. Later, he joined the Golden State Warriors in 2017 and helped them win the NBA title in 2018. Then, in 2018, he was traded to the Denver Nuggets.
