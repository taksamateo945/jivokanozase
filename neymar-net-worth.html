Neymar’s Salary

Neymar, a Brazilian footballer, began his career with Santos FC and later joined FC Barcelona.

He won several titles and awards, including the Copa Libertadores in 2011.

In 2017, he transferred to Paris Saint-Germain for €222 million, becoming the most expensive player ever.

In 2023, he joined Al-Hilal for €90 million, becoming the highest-paid footballer in the world and possibly the highest-paid athlete on the planet in terms of base salary.

Neymar’s Endorsements

Neymar, a footballer, has made significant income from endorsements and sponsorships, earning $18 million in 2020.

Neymar’s wealth is not solely derived from his salary, but also from his endorsements and sponsorships.
