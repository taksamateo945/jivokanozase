Brandy Norwood Announced That She Was Rushed To Hospital After She Got A Stroke

 Posted on February 12, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  477 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

On October 11, reports about Brandy Norwood’s hospitalization began to circulate on the internet. As fans were worried, the singer recently updated her health status on her Instagram Story. She stated:

“Thank you for sending love and light my way, cherished family, friends, and stars.” I’m following physicians’ directions and obtaining the rest I need due to dehydration and a lack of nutrients. Thank you for your prayers and encouragement. Thank you everybody, and I hope to see you soon.”

Norwood’s agent has yet to issue a formal comment.

Brandy Norwood was allegedly hospitalized after having a seizure. According to TMZ, officials received an EMS call to Norwood’s home around noon on October 11. TMZ also said that her parents were in the hospital with her. The causes of the seizure and the confirmation of additional health conditions are yet unknown. It should be mentioned that she was admitted to the hospital in 2017 after losing consciousness on a flight at Los Angeles International Airport.

Norwood’s staff said at the time that she was traveling extensively as part of her tour, taking more than ten flights. They also noted that Norwood had been working all night in her studio before leaving for the airport to catch a 5:45 a.m. flight. In an interview with the New York Post, she stated:

“I haven’t been classified as bipolar, but I’ve had instances when trauma has forced me to be less than myself, and I thought at one point that I could’ve experienced that.”

In a separate interview with The Grio, she revealed that she had struggled with severe depression, trauma, and PTSD. She went on to say that she had been through a lot and had to conquer it all via counseling, prayer, writing, and meditation.

All About  Brandy Norwood

Brandy Norwood, 43, has sold around 40 million records worldwide, including 8.62 million albums in the United States. In 1993, she signed with Atlantic Records and released her self-titled first album the following year. She made her acting debut in the comedy Moesha, and since then has appeared in films such as Cinderella and I Still Know What You Did Last Summer.

She rose to prominence with her song “The Boy Is Mine,” and in 2002, she starred in the reality series Brandy: Special Delivery. Her third album, Full Moon, was published in 2002, and her fourth album, Afrodisiac, was released in 2004. She was a judge on America’s Got Talent’s inaugural season.

Human, her fifth album, was published in 2008, and she appeared on Dancing with the Stars in 2010. She has also featured in series such as The Game, Two Eleven, Zoe Ever After, and others. Her seventh studio album, B7, was released in 2020, and in 2021, she starred in the music drama series Queens.
