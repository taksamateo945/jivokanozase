Javonte Williams Parents & Family Background

Javonte was born to parents Jermaine Williams and Shekemia Williams on 25 April 2000. He is 22 years old now.

The athlete spent his early life in Wallace, North Carolina. His father Jermaine was a barber and used to run a bail bonds company.

Williams’ father has been playing an influential role in his career since his high school days. He shares a close bond and the same nickname, “Pookie,” with his father. He is the eldest child in the family and has a baby sister.

His NFL Journey

Williams attended Wallace-Rose Hill High School in Teachey, North Carolina where he played high school football. Afterward, he committed to the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill to play college football.

The 5-foot-10-inch played for Tar Heels for three seasons. He ended his collegiate career, rushing 366 times for 2,297 yards with 29 touchdowns and catching 50 passes for 539 yards with four scores.


Javonte was drafted by the Denver Broncos in the second round 35th overall of the 2021 NFL Draft. On 12 September 2021, he made his NFL debut in the Week 1 game against the New York Giants.
