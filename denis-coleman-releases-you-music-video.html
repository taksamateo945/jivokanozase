Denis Coleman Releases Music Video For New Single 'You'

 Posted on June 13, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  197 words  |  Larita Shotwell

15 year old pop protege Denis Coleman is the popstar in the making that you need in your life. He has been honing his musical craftsmanship from the age of 4 and the multi-instrumentalist even wrote his first original song aged 10.

Denis has recently been part of The Power of Musik’s schools tour across the UK, a new program created by celebrity vocal coach and mentor CeCe Sammy, fashioned to empower and inspire young people through music education.

Now, he looks to cement himself as a serious musician with the unveiling of his single and music video ‘You’. The track was written entirely by himself and focuses on the feelings attached to young romance. Fun, upbeat and catchy, ‘You’ is a stellar track full of potential.

The video is reminiscent of a young Justin Bieber and/or Cody Simpson and highlights Denis’ ability to capture his audience. Centre stage, all eyes are on Denis and his sheer talent. ‘You’ is pure pop at its finest, with an infectious melody complemented by Denis’ youthful voice and lyrics.
