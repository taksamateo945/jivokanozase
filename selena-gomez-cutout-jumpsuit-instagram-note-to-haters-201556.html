Selena Gomez Rocks a Cutout Jumpsuit, Calls Out Haters on Instagram

 Posted on June 16, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  297 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Working that sidewalk like a model!

Proving that she is just as much of a street-style queen as her bestie Taylor Swift, Selena Gomez stepped out to grab one of Jamba Juice’s green smoothies in a sizzling ensemble on Thursday, June 4, in Los Angeles.

The “I Want You to Know” songstress, 22, chose a slate, denim Free People jumpsuit with cutout details up each side for her healthy snack break. She tied a blue bandana around her neck like a choker, and walked tall in quilted stiletto booties.

As for her radiant beauty choices, Gomez modeled a ruby red pout, bronzed skin, and lush lashes during the jaunt, not to mention her mega-watt smile between laughs. (See more photos of Gomez on X17online.com.)

The Disney alum truly seemed in good spirits while out and about—and it’s no wonder! That same day, she hopped onto Instagram to announce, “I want to claim my own name,” complete with a message to “them fools” who have tried to tear her down in the past.

“Sometimes I get frustrated when I hear lies about who I am. The Internet is meant to keep you ‘updated’ but it just takes old news and freezes it like it’s now,” Gomez began. “You have watched me in pain and I’ve owned up to it through my music and actions. I grew up with you. I am beyond excited to show you the next chapter, the TRUTH.”
